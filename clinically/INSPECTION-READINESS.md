# Inspection readiness — the checklist

Evidence base: [CQC-INSPECTION-INSIGHTS.md](CQC-INSPECTION-INSIGHTS.md) (real CQC registration interview). Content model: [POLICY-CORPUS.md](POLICY-CORPUS.md). Spec: [CLINICKLY-MASTER.md](CLINICKLY-MASTER.md) §5.7.

## What the feature does
For each clinic, Clinickly renders this checklist **personalised to their service and regulator(s)**, and for every question answers three things:

1. **Can you answer this?**
2. **Which of your documents evidences it?** — auto-linked from the requirement-theme map
3. **Is that document signed and current?**

Output: **"You have no answer for 4 questions"** + one-click routes to fix each (build the missing policy · record the missing risk assessment · assign the missing training).

> **Compliance support, not certification — the registered manager decides.** A readiness aid based on what a real inspector asked; it does not guarantee a registration or inspection outcome.

## The five tests behind every question
Every item below exists because the inspector applied one of these:
1. **"Which policy is that in?"** — every claim traceable to a named document
2. **"But the policies don't say that"** — practice and policy must match
3. **"Do your staff know?"** — a policy nobody has read isn't a control
4. **"Is that written down anywhere?"** — every *opt-out* needs a documented risk assessment
5. **"How have you personalised these?"** — generic/bought-in policies are actively probed

## PREMISES WALKTHROUGH — physical checks
- Reception and waiting area · consultation room layout · patient privacy
- **Wipeable/vinyl surfaces**, sharps box placement, cleaning equipment
- **Emergency kit, item by item** — incl. **paediatric strengths** of adrenaline auto-injectors and **paediatric defibrillator pads** *(a real gap found in this interview)*
- **Accessibility adaptations** — wheelchair access, grab handles *(also a real gap found)*
- Waste storage and disposal route

## Build notes
- Personalise per clinic: **regulator(s) selected** drive which questions appear; service answers drive the conditional ones (CDs, children, procedures, remote).
- **Auto-link evidence** via the requirement-theme map — never ask the clinic to map it manually.
- Flag a question as unanswered if the linked document is **missing, unsigned, or overdue review**.
- Exportable as the **inspection pack**: checklist + linked evidence + Master Policy Index + audit trail.
