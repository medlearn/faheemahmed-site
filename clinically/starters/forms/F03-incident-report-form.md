> **Reporting is expected and is never held against you.** No one is disciplined for reporting an incident promptly and honestly. This form exists to fix systems, not to find fault.
>
> **Report near misses too.** Something that nearly went wrong is free information about what will go wrong next time.
>
> **Complete within one working day.** If anyone is at risk right now, deal with that first and tell `[CONFIRM: role]` immediately — don't wait to fill this in.
