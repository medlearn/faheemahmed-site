## How to use this form

**The conversation is the consent. This form is the record of it.**

A signature on a form where no discussion happened is worth nothing, and a well-documented discussion with no signature is worth a great deal. Complete the discussion section fully — it is the part that would answer a question years later.

**One form per procedure or treatment course.** Where a patient consents to several things, use a separate form for each, or list them separately with separate signatures.

## Patient statement

**Please read this before signing.**

- I have had the treatment explained to me, and I have had the chance to ask questions.
- I understand the benefits, the risks that matter to me, and the alternatives — including choosing not to have it.
- I understand what it will cost.
- **I understand I can change my mind at any time, including after signing this form, and that this will not affect the care I receive here.**
- I understand that no guarantee has been given about the result.
