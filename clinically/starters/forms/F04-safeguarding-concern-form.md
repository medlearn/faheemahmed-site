> ## Before you write anything
>
> **If anyone is in immediate danger — call 999 now.** Complete this afterwards.
>
> **Complete this the same day, while it is fresh.** A contemporaneous record in the person's own words is worth far more later than an accurate summary written next week.
>
> **Your job is to record and report. It is not to investigate.** Do not question the person in detail, do not confront anyone, and do not promise to keep it secret.
>
> **Doing nothing is not an option.** Never assume someone else will pass it on.
>
> **Consent is not required to make a safeguarding referral.** Where a person objects, record it — and refer anyway if the threshold is met.
