## Known gaps / caveats
- **Dated March 2022** — overdue by the corpus's own two-year review rule; every entry needs updating to current standards before publication.
- **Ahmeys-specific content** (named directors, Oxford premises, "three minutes' walk from Ahmeys Pharmacy") must be de-personalised to `[CONFIRM: …]` placeholders — facts become placeholders, process text stays.
- **Format is not uniform** — only ~55 of 119 carry an explicit `AIM` section; the standard house format (version block → References → Scope → AIM → POLICY → PROCEDURE) must be applied consistently on re-authoring.
- Some documents are stubs (CT06 14 words, HR10/HR11 <20 words) — forms rather than policies.
