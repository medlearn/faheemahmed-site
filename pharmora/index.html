Our specialism

Pharmacy is our specialism, not a sideline.

A pharmacy is not an ordinary business. It answers to the GPhC, it may answer to the CQC, it operates under an NHS contract, and it employs registered professionals whose standing matters as much as its turnover. Decisions that look simple on paper — a change of ownership, taking on staff from overseas, restructuring finance — carry regulatory weight that a generalist adviser will miss.

Pharmora exists to close that gap. We bring business, financial and legal expertise that is already grounded in how pharmacy actually works, so you are never paying someone to learn your sector on your time.
