Meet your doctor

Dr Aya Mohamed

Dr Aya Mohamed is a UK medical doctor with over 10 years of clinical experience and a specialist interest in dermatology and aesthetic medicine. She currently works as a Locum Consultant Dermatologist within the NHS, alongside her private aesthetic practice.

With extensive experience managing a wide range of skin conditions in both NHS and private settings, Dr Aya combines medical expertise with a meticulous aesthetic eye. Her approach is centred on achieving natural, balanced results while maintaining the highest standards of patient safety and clinical care.

Whether treating acne, rosacea and pigmentation, assessing moles and skin lesions, or providing advanced facial rejuvenation, every treatment plan is tailored to the individual. Dr Aya believes that the best aesthetic outcomes are those that enhance your natural features rather than change them.

Patients value her honest advice, thorough consultations and calm, unhurried approach. Every consultation and treatment is performed personally by Dr Aya, ensuring continuity of care from your first appointment through to your follow-up.
