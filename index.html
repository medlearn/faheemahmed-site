About

A clinician who teaches the way he wishes he'd been taught.

I'm Faheem Ahmed — an educator, author and consultant working across healthcare and education, and the voice behind The Pharmacy Guy. My work sits where clinical practice, education and enterprise meet: helping clinicians, teams and organisations turn knowledge into safe, confident, real-world practice.

I teach and design clinical training, write books and papers, advise healthcare ventures, and develop specialist services across healthcare. I care a great deal about getting the detail right and staying clear about where the evidence stops.
